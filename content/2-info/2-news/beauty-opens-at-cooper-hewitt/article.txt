Text: “Beauty—Cooper Hewitt Design Triennial” opens today, featuring models and images from Amant and Veiled.  Museum Director, Caroline Baumann says, “The exhibition will celebrate design as a creative endeavor that engages the mind, body and senses with works of astonishing form and surprising function.” The exhibition runs from February 12 through August 21.

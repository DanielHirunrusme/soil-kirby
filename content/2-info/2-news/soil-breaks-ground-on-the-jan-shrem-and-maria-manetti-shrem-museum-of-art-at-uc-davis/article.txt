Title: SO–IL breaks ground on the Jan Shrem and Maria Manetti Shrem Museum of Art at UC Davis

----

Coverimage: 

----

Date: 2014-03-06

----

Text: 

SO–IL announces the groundbreaking on March 1, 2014 for the new Jan Shrem and Maria Manetti Shrem Museum of Art at the University of California, Davis in Davis, CA. Designed by SO–IL, in a design-build partnership with Bohlin Cywinski Jackson and Whiting-Turner, the design advances the fundamental notion of the art museum in the 21st century.
